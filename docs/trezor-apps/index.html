    TREZOR Apps¶

    Except for TREZOR Wallet, you can use your TREZOR device with increasing number of independent bitcoin wallets and online services. We call them TREZOR Apps:

    • TREZOR Wallets
    • Online Services
    • Recovering Funds

    Using TREZOR With Bitcoin and Altcoin Wallets¶

    The following client wallets let you connect TREZOR and work with it. They don’t actually hold any private keys, instead they display balances and help you to manage transactions.

    Using TREZOR With Online Services¶

    Following websites allow you a secure password-less Sign in with TREZOR or other great bitcoin related services, e.g. creating invoices from your TREZOR accounts. Click on the name of a service for more details.

    Service Feature(s) Description
    Bitex Real time bitcoin exchange.
    Bitstamp Real time bitcoin exchange.
    Bitwala Payment processor. Pay your Euro bills with Bitcoin.
    Cashila Payment processor. Pay your Euro bills with Bitcoin.
    Coinmap Map of bitcoin shops and businesses.
    Coinpayments Payment processor service supporting various cryptocurrencies.
    Osclass Open-source platform for easy creation of classifieds sites.
    Strip4Bit Adult site allowing users to sign up privately in a secure way.
    Wordpress Blog hosting and publishing platform.

    LEGEND | Sign in with TREZOR | Sign up with TREZOR | Move funds directly from/to TREZOR

    Recovering Funds without TREZOR Device¶

    In case your TREZOR device gets lost or damaged, you can access your bitcoins fast using your paper backup and one of the following wallets:

    • ArcBit (iOS)
    • Copay (Linux, Windows, OSX, Android, iOS, Windows phone)
    • Electrum (Linux, Windows, OSX, Android), recovery manual
    • MultiBit HD (Linux, Windows, OSX) - only the first account can be accessed, passphrase not supported
    • Mycelium (Android), recovery manual
